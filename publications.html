Ilya Ash (b. 1986 in Moscow, Russia) is a poet and journalist. He served as a website editor and literary news broadcaster for Echo of Moscow radio station (2010-2015) and as a senior editor for Discours online magazine (2019-2020), both of which were later banned by Russian authorities. He has also contributed to a variety of other publications, such as Esquire Kazakhstan, Booknik.ru, and Colta.ru. Ilya's poetry has been published in various literary outlets, including TextOnly, Dvoetochie, Articulation, and Polutona. His double feature “Бесполезные песни / Лилипутское порно. Две книги стихотворений” (“Worthless Songs / Midget Porn. Two books of poems”) is due to be published by Tango Whiskyman in 2023.

Не is currently based in Jerusalem, Israel.
